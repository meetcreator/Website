Practical Training

Articleship Positions

Mehta & Associates is registered as a training establishment under ICAI's Practical Training guidelines. Articled assistants work across all practice areas — income tax, GST, audit, and company law — under direct partner supervision.

Training at the firm exposes articled assistants to a wide range of client types and compliance scenarios. We prioritise quality of learning over volume of work.

What to expect

Rotation across income tax, GST, audit, and ROC compliance
Supervised preparation of returns, audit reports, and client correspondence
Attendance at departmental hearings and assessments (where applicable)
Access to ICAI CPE sessions and firm-internal knowledge sharing
Stipend as per ICAI minimum prescribed rates
