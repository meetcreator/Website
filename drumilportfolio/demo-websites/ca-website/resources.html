Reference Articles

Brief reference notes on common compliance topics. These are general overviews — specific advice should be sought from a qualified professional.

Income Tax March 2025

Understanding the New Tax Regime (Section 115BAC)

The new tax regime is now default from FY 2023-24. Individuals must actively opt out to claim deductions like 80C, HRA, and home loan interest under the old regime.

GST February 2025

ITC Eligibility and Blocked Credits under Section 17(5)

Not all GST paid on purchases is creditable. Section 17(5) blocks ITC on motor vehicles, food & beverages, club memberships, and certain construction expenditure.

Company Law January 2025

Private Limited vs. LLP: Choosing the Right Structure

LLPs offer operational flexibility and lower compliance cost; Private Limited companies allow easier equity investment. The choice depends on long-term business intent and funding plans.

NRI Taxation December 2024

NRI Status and Indian Income Tax Obligations

Residency under the Income Tax Act is determined by physical presence — not domicile or passport. NRIs are taxed only on Indian-source income; DTAA benefits may reduce withholding.

Audit November 2024

Tax Audit Applicability under Section 44AB

A tax audit is required if business turnover exceeds ₹1 Cr (or ₹10 Cr if cash transactions are below 5%). Professionals must get audited if gross receipts exceed ₹50 lakhs.

FEMA October 2024

Form 15CA / 15CB — When Is a CA Certificate Needed?

Foreign remittances above specified thresholds require Form 15CA (online declaration) and Form 15CB (CA certificate) confirming TDS deduction or treaty exemption.

All articles are for general reference only. Law provisions are as understood at the time of writing and may have been amended. Please consult a professional for advice specific to your situation.
