Privacy Policy

This policy explains what limited information this website collects and how it is handled.

Last updated: June 2025  |  Applies to: www.mehtaandassociates.in [TO BE UPDATED]

1. Who we are

Mehta & Associates, Chartered Accountants is a partnership firm registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Our registered office is in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. References to "the firm", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Mehta & Associates.

2. What information this website collects

This website is a static informational site. We collect:

  • Enquiry form submissions — when you use the contact form on this site, we receive the name, phone number, email address, and message you submit. This information is transmitted to the firm's email inbox.
  • Standard server logs — our web host may log IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited as part of routine server operation. We do not access or analyse these logs for marketing purposes.

We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics), advertising pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts. The only third-party content on this site is:

  • Google Fonts — loaded from Google's CDN for typography (may result in a request to Google's servers from your browser).
  • Unsplash image CDN — images served from Unsplash's servers for illustrative photography.
  • OpenStreetMap embed on the Contact page — subject to OSM's privacy policy.

3. How we use the information

Information submitted via the enquiry form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and establish whether we can assist you.
  • Correspond with you about a potential or existing professional engagement.

We do not use your contact details for marketing, newsletters, or any unsolicited communication. We are bound by ICAI's Code of Ethics, which prohibits solicitation.

4. Disclosure of information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except where required by law (e.g., court order, regulatory requirement) or with your explicit consent.

5. Data retention

Enquiry emails are retained in the firm's email system for a reasonable period to support correspondence. If you proceed to a formal engagement, relevant records are maintained in accordance with professional obligations under the Income Tax Act, Companies Act, and ICAI guidelines, which may require retention for specified periods (typically 8 years for tax-related records).

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have submitted to us (subject to any legal retention requirements) by writing to us at info@mehtaandassociates.in.

7. Security

This website does not store your data on its own servers. Enquiry form data is transmitted by email. We take reasonable precautions to secure our email infrastructure; however, no transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Links to external sites

This site may link to external websites (e.g., ICAI, GSTN, MCA). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
