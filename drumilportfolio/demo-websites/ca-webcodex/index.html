Services Snapshot

Core work, stated plainly.

The firm handles recurring compliance and transaction-linked advisory where documentation, due dates and professional judgement matter.

01

Income Tax and GST

Returns, planning notes, assessments, appeals, registrations and reconciliations.

02

Audit and Assurance

Statutory audit, internal audit, tax audit and process observations for management.

03

Entity and ROC Compliance

Company and LLP incorporation, annual filings, board records and event-based forms.

04

Accounting, Payroll and Advisory

Bookkeeping, MIS, payroll compliance, NRI taxation, RBI/FEMA and business advisory.

Compliance work should be predictable, documented and calm enough for management to make decisions without last-minute uncertainty.

Why Work With Us

Process before presentation.

Documented positions. Advice is recorded with working notes, references and assumptions where required.

Due-date discipline. Recurring work is tracked through compliance calendars and internal review points.

Partner involvement. Matters involving judgement, notices or representation are reviewed by a partner.

Restrained communication. The firm avoids comparative claims and keeps public communication factual.
