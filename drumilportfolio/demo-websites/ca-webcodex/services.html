Services

Tax, compliance and advisory work for Indian businesses.

The following services are subject to engagement scope, independence requirements and applicable professional standards.

01

Income Tax

Return filing, tax planning notes, scrutiny assessment support, appeal documentation and litigation coordination.

02

GST

Registration, monthly and annual returns, input tax credit reconciliation, audit support and notice replies.

03

Company and LLP

Incorporation, ROC annual compliance, statutory registers, board records and event-based filings.

04

Statutory and Internal Audit

Audit planning, evidence review, reporting, internal controls review and management observations.

Salary processing, TDS, PF/ESI coordination and employee reimbursement records.

NRI Taxation

Residential status review, Indian income reporting, capital gains computation and withholding guidance.

08

RBI and FEMA

Foreign remittance documentation, FDI reporting support, ODI records and compliance review.

09

Business Advisory

Entity structuring notes, cash-flow review, accounting process design and transaction documentation.
