Our Journey

From a single desk
to 80+ countries.

What started as a small commodities trading desk in Dubai has evolved into a full-spectrum global trade company with offices in 14 cities and partnerships on 6 continents.

2012

Founded in Dubai

Started as a commodity export firm focusing on the GCC–South Asia corridor.

2015

Expanded to Europe & Africa

Opened Rotterdam and Nairobi offices; grew to 12 active trade lanes.

2018

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Launched digital shipment tracking platform; 500+ active clients.

2021

Americas & Asia Pacific

Established North America and Southeast Asia divisions; 10,000+ shipments milestone.

2024

80+ Countries & Counting

350+ trade professionals worldwide, 15,000+ completed shipments, 98% on-time.
