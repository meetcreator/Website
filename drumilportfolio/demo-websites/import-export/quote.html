FAQ

Common questions
about our quote process.

How long does it take to receive a quote?

Standard quotes are delivered within 24 business hours. Complex multi-leg shipments or hazardous cargo may take up to 48 hours.

Is the quote binding?

Initial quotes are indicative. Final binding rates are confirmed after verifying exact cargo specs, current carrier rates, and port conditions.

What information do I need to prepare?

Origin and destination, commodity description, weight & dimensions, HS code (if known), and preferred timeline. The more detail, the faster and more accurate your quote.

Can I get quotes for recurring shipments?

Yes. We offer long-term rate agreements for regular lanes. Contact our team directly for dedicated account pricing.
