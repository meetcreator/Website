About the Firm

Independent practice with documented working methods.

Rao & Mehta Chartered Accountants was established in 2012 to provide tax, audit and accounting services to privately held Indian businesses and professionals.

2012

Formation

The practice began with tax filing, audit support and bookkeeping assignments for local businesses.

2017

GST Transition

The firm added registration, return and reconciliation support as GST became part of routine compliance.

2021 onwards

Digital Records

Workflows expanded to include cloud accounting review, payroll records and structured MIS packs.

Mission

To make statutory compliance clear, timely and properly evidenced.

The firm's work is guided by professional independence, documentation, confidentiality and the ICAI Code of Ethics. Public communication is kept factual and does not include comparative claims, client endorsements or unverified performance statements.

Values: independence in judgement, care in documentation, punctuality in compliance and confidentiality in client records.
