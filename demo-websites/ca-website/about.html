Who We Are

About the Firm

Over two and a half decades of practising Chartered Accountancy in Gujarat, built on continuity, professional ethics, and a focused approach to client service.

Our Story

A practise built
over generations.

Mehta & Associates was founded in 1998 by CA Rajesh Mehta in Ahmedabad. What began as a sole proprietorship focused on income tax compliance has grown into a full-service CA firm with three partners and twelve professional staff.

The firm has maintained consistent growth through a deliberate policy of limiting client intake to ensure service quality. We do not seek rapid expansion; instead, we prioritise long-term, substantive engagements with a manageable client base.

All partners hold full ICAI membership and continue professional education commitments as required under ICAI's CPE framework.

Firm History

Key Milestones

1998

Founding

2004

GST readiness & indirect tax practice

The firm expands its VAT and Service Tax advisory team, subsequently transitioning to GST advisory from 2017.

2016

Third partner inducted

CA Ankit D. Shah joins as partner, leading the NRI taxation and FEMA compliance vertical.

2023

Current practice

The firm currently operates with three partners, twelve staff, and serves clients across Gujarat, Maharashtra, and internationally.

Our Mission

What guides our work.

To provide Chartered Accountancy services of consistent professional quality, advising clients within the bounds of applicable law and ICAI's Code of Ethics — with no compromise on independence or confidentiality.

We measure our work by the quality of advice, the accuracy of compliance filings, and the professional relationships we maintain — not by revenue targets or client count.

Professional Values

What we stand by.

Integrity

We will not accommodate advice that contravenes applicable law, regardless of commercial pressure.

Objectivity

Our professional judgement is not influenced by personal interest or third-party bias.

Confidentiality

Client information is never disclosed without specific authorisation or legal obligation to do so.

Competence

We maintain the technical knowledge required to serve our clients, with ongoing CPE compliance under ICAI's framework.
