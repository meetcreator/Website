Direct Taxation

Income Tax

We assist individuals, Hindu Undivided Families, partnership firms, LLPs, and companies across the full income tax compliance and advisory cycle — from routine annual filings to complex assessment proceedings.

Income tax return preparation and filing (all ITR forms)
Advance tax computation and deposit advisory
TDS / TCS compliance — computation, challans, returns (24Q/26Q/27Q/27EQ)
Tax planning and restructuring within applicable law
Representation before Assessing Officers (scrutiny / assessment)
Appeals before CIT(A) and ITAT
Rectification applications, refund follow-up
Lower / nil TDS certificate applications (Section 195, 197)
Form 15CA / 15CB for foreign remittances

Indirect Taxation

GST Advisory & Compliance

From initial registration to complex classification disputes, our GST practice covers the full scope of obligations under the Goods and Services Tax framework.

GST registration (regular, composition, casual taxable person)
Monthly / quarterly return filing — GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-4
Annual return — GSTR-9 and reconciliation statement GSTR-9C
Input tax credit review and reconciliation with GSTR-2A / 2B
GST audit (where applicable under State Acts)
Classification and rate advisory
Departmental enquiry response and notice handling
E-way bill compliance advisory
Refund applications — exports, inverted duty structure

Corporate Law

Company & LLP Incorporation and Compliance

We assist in setting up legal entities and maintaining their statutory records and filings under the Companies Act 2013 and LLP Act 2008.

Private limited company incorporation — MCA21 filings, MOA/AOA drafting
LLP registration — LLP agreement, designated partner KYC
OPC (One Person Company) incorporation
Annual ROC filings — AOC-4 (financial statements), MGT-7 (annual return)
Board resolutions, statutory registers maintenance
Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC), DIN allotment
Change of registered office, change in share capital
Strike-off / closure procedures (Form STK-2)

Cross-Border Compliance

NRI Taxation & FEMA / RBI Compliance

Non-resident Indians and foreign investors face a distinct set of tax and regulatory obligations. We handle both the income tax and FEMA dimensions.

NRI income tax return filing (Indian-source income)
Residency status determination (RNOR, NRI, Resident analysis)
Double taxation avoidance agreement (DTAA) advisory
Repatriation of funds — NRE / NRO / FCNR account advisory
FEMA compliance — reporting of foreign assets, overseas investments
RBI approvals — prior permission filings, compounding applications

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) reporting — FC-GPR, FC-TRS
