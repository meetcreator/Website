About The Program

What are Higher Order Thinking Skills?

Higher Order Thinking Skills go beyond rote learning and memorization. They prepare children to become confident learners, innovators, and future-ready thinkers.

  • Think critically
  • Solve problems
  • Observe carefully
  • Make decisions
  • Express creativity
  • Analyze situations
  • Apply learning in real-life contexts

Program Structure

Four Cognitive Domains

Aligned with Bloom's Taxonomy, each domain builds progressively across Nursery, Jr. KG, and Sr. KG.

1.

🧠 Remembering

"I Can Recall and Recognize"

Skills Included

This level develops a child's ability to remember, identify, and recall information.

  • Recognizing objects
  • Naming pictures
  • Recalling facts
  • Identifying colors, shapes, numbers, and letters
  • Remembering sequences

Class-Wise Focus

Nursery

Identify animals, fruits, colors · Recall rhymes and sounds

Jr. KG

Recognize patterns and symbols · Recall simple instructions

Sr. KG

Remember story details · Recall number concepts and vocabulary

Sample Questions

👉"What color is the apple?"
👉"Can you name this animal?"
👉"What comes after 5?"
2.

💡 Understanding

"I Can Explain and Understand"

Skills Included

This level checks whether children understand meaning and can explain ideas in simple ways.

  • Explaining ideas
  • Matching and classifying
  • Comparing objects
  • Following instructions
  • Understanding relationships

Class-Wise Focus

Nursery

Match related objects · Understand simple concepts like big-small

Jr. KG

Compare objects · Explain everyday situations

Sr. KG

Sequence events · Explain why something happens

Sample Questions

👉"Why do we wear raincoats?"
👉"Which objects belong together?"
👉"How are these two pictures different?"
3.

🔍 Analysing

"I Can Think and Solve"

Skills Included

This level develops logical reasoning, observation, and problem-solving abilities.

  • Finding patterns
  • Identifying differences
  • Sequencing
  • Problem-solving
  • Cause-and-effect reasoning
  • Decision making

Class-Wise Focus

Nursery

Spot the difference · Complete simple patterns

Jr. KG

Identify odd one out · Arrange pictures in order

Sr. KG

Solve situational problems · Predict outcomes · Analyze picture scenarios

Sample Questions

👉"Which picture does not belong?"
👉"What will happen next?"
👉"How can we solve this problem?"
4.

🎨 Creativity

"I Can Imagine and Create"

Skills Included

This level encourages imagination, innovation, and original thinking.

  • Creative drawing
  • Story creation
  • Open-ended thinking
  • Multiple solutions
  • Imaginative responses

Class-Wise Focus

Nursery

Complete picture drawing · Pretend play ideas

Jr. KG

Create endings to stories · Think of new uses for objects

Sr. KG

Design solutions · Invent ideas · Express independent thinking

Sample Questions

👉"Draw what you see in the sky."
👉"What can you make using a box?"
👉"Imagine you could fly — where would you go?"
